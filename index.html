Expression and methylation are associated with caste and age
Many of the modules gene co-expression and co-methylation modules were correlated with either caste (outer band), or developmetal stage (middle band) or their interaction (inner band). Significant correlations with the queen caste and developmental maturity are highlighted in red , while worker and newly eclosed developmental stage correlations are highlighted in blue . Significant caste by developmental stage interactions are highlighted in purple .

Although conservation of modules between co-expression and co-methylation datasets was generally weak, four expression modules (E1, E6, E9 and E10) were conserved in the co-methylation data (See Table S16). Their connections are highlighted in orange .
